## Notes

- /bag and /profile balance pulled forward from Phase 3 scope (confirmed not explicitly assigned there; directly supports 02.1 craft loop)
- Unique item design change: craft no longer takes name/emoji inputs; unique output is the per-profession seeded mystery item (e.g. "Vô Danh Đan"). Appraisal/naming deferred to future "giám định" phase.
- Seed must be re-run on live DB after deploy: `npx tsx src/db/seed.ts` (idempotent via ON CONFLICT DO UPDATE)
- Craft probability formula: base 40% fail at tier 1 / realm 0 / profLevel 0. tierPenalty adds 10% per tier above 1. getMajorRealmIndex() reduces fail by 3% per major realm. Defensive clamp ensures success ≥ 0% always.
- ⚠️ Tier 7+ items: successRate = 0% at realm 0 regardless of profLevel. Need minRealmId gate in recipes table before seeding tier 7+ items (see itemAttributes.ts JSDoc warning).
